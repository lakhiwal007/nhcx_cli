Project Implementation Summary

1. Backend & System Capabilities (Implemented)
--------------------------------------------------
1. View Cashless Claim Summary
   - Core logic to aggregate total claims, status counts, and preauth pending counts.
2. View Patients With Claims
   - Ability to identify unique patients with active workflows.
3. Patient Registry Integration
   - Search by child ID, name, or mobile; fetch latest claim status.
4. Payer Registry Integration
   - Search participant codes for the NHCX network.
5. Policy Fetch (Coverage Eligibility)
   - Integration with payer to retrieve available policies for a patient.
6. Cashless Workflow Engine
   - Starts insurance plan (IP) and coverage eligibility (CE) checks.
7. Tracking Engine
   - Aggregates status for procedures, benefits, and authorization requirements.
8. Preauth Generation
   - Loads hospital data (diagnoses, procedures, bill items, care team) into preauth drafts.
9. Preauth Submission
   - Handles the handshake with payers and tracking via correlation IDs.
10. Preauth Status Monitoring
    - Polls for adjudication decisions and amount breakdowns.
11. Resubmission/Cancellation Logic
    - Core logic to handle rejections or user-driven cancellations.


2 Frontend UI/UX Screens (Using mock data)
---------------------------------------------
1. Dashboard Screen
   - Aggregate statistics (Total, Pending, Completed claims).
   - Patient Registry search by Name or Mobile.
   - List of patients with ID, Gender, DOB, and current NHCX status.
2. Payer & Policy Selection
   - Searchable directory of NHCX payers.
   - Fetching and displaying available policies for a specific patient.
   - Policy details: Product name, coverage limits, and validity.
3. Cashless Preparation
   - Tracking progress of Insurance Plan (IP) and Coverage Eligibility (CE) requests.
   - Displaying procedure details and medical diagnoses.
   - Benefit breakdowns: Limit, Used, and Auth requirements.
   - Inclusions/Exclusions and required preauth documents.
4. Preauth Review Screen
   - Comprehensive summary of clinical and financial data before submission.
   - Diagnosis codes (ICD-10) and Procedure codes.
   - Line-item breakdown of service costs.
5. Preauth Status Tracking
   - Real-time status of submitted preauths (Approved/Rejected/Query).
   - Correlation IDs and Payer reference numbers.
   - Error messages and notes from the payer.




3. Pending Use Cases
-----------------------------------
1. Claim List View
Dashboard summary exists, but a full claim list/table for all cashless cases is not finalized.

2. Claim Detail Page
Individual cashless case status exists, but a complete detail page flow is still pending.

3. Filters And Sorting
Pending filters include status, payer, date range, patient, preauth decision, and facility.

4. Document Upload Flow
Supporting documents can be passed, but dashboard document upload/management flow is not finalized.

5. Final Claim / Discharge Flow
Cashless and preauth flow is covered. Final claim submission, discharge, claim query response, claim resubmission, and claim status are not part of the dashboard flow yet.

6. Payment Tracking
Payment acknowledgement/status exists internally, but dashboard payment tracking is not exposed in this frontend dashboard flow yet.

7. Multi-Facility Dashboard
Architecture supports facilities, but dashboard-level facility filters and facility-wise stats are pending.
